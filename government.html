Tenders & Bidding Process

We actively participate in government tenders through transparent and competitive bidding, ensuring adherence to the latest procurement norms.

Tender Participation Process:

  • Registration on Government Portals:
    • Central Public Procurement Portal (CPPP)
    • Government e-Marketplace (GeM)
    • Indian Railways e-Procurement System (IREPS)
  • Bid Submission & Documentation:
    • Financial & technical proposal preparation.
    • Earnest Money Deposit (EMD) & bid security submission.
    • Submission of past project credentials & experience.
  • Government Contract Execution:
    • Compliance with contractual obligations.
    • Timely execution & reporting as per project milestones.
    • Adherence to Public Procurement Policy (Make in India) guidelines.

Regulatory Standards

Indian regulatory frameworks and environmental guidelines are strictly followed to ensure ethical and sustainable construction in our projects.

Key Compliance Areas:

  • Construction Laws & Regulations:
    • The Building and Other Construction Workers Act, 1996
    • The Indian Contract Act, 1872
  • Environmental Compliance:
    • Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) under the Environment Protection Act, 1986
    • Pollution Control Norms – CPCB Guidelines
  • Infrastructure-Specific Regulations:
    • The Railways Act, 1989
    • NHAI Guidelines
    • DGCA (Directorate General of Civil Aviation) Norms

Quality & Safety Assurance

Ensuring compliance with globally recognized standards, we prioritize delivering high-quality, durable, and safe infrastructure.

Key Certifications & Safety Standards:

  • ISO 9001:2015 – Quality Management System
  • ISO 14001:2015 – Environmental Management System
  • ISO 45001:2018 – Occupational Health & Safety Management System
  • BIS (Bureau of Indian Standards) Certification
  • OSHA (Occupational Safety and Health Administration) Guidelines
  • RERA (Real Estate Regulatory Authority) Compliance
